# ShortReels Facebook Marketing Playbook

## 🎯 Target Audience

### Primary (Age 16-28)
- Interests: TikTok, memes, comedy, AI, short videos, Netflix
- Behaviors: Mobile app users, entertainment seekers, social media active
- Locations: US, UK, Australia, Canada (English-speaking first)

### Secondary (Age 28-40)
- Interests: Technology, AI tools, digital entertainment
- Behaviors: Podcast listeners, YouTube subscribers, app early adopters

## 💰 Budget Recommendations

| Phase | Daily Budget | Duration | Goal |
|-------|-------------|----------|------|
| Test | $5-10/day | 7 days | Find winning creative |
| Scale | $20-50/day | 14 days | Drive installs at <$1 CPI |
| Grow | $50-100/day | Ongoing | Scale what works |

## 📊 Key Metrics to Track
- **CPI (Cost Per Install):** Target <$1.50
- **Trial Start Rate:** Target >30% of installs
- **Paid Conversion:** Target >10% of trials
- **Day 7 Retention:** Target >25%

## 🎬 Creative Specs

### Facebook Feed Video
- Resolution: 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1920 (vertical)
- Length: 15-30 seconds
- Format: MP4, max 4GB
- Show a skit playing with a funny punchline

### Instagram Stories
- Resolution: 1080x1920 (vertical)
- Length: 15 seconds max
- Show quick skit clip with "Swipe Up to Watch More"

### Instagram Reels
- Use actual skit clips as Reels content (organic + boosted)
- Caption: "This skit was made by AI 🤯 Watch more on ShortReels"

## 🔥 Organic Growth Tactics (Free)

### TikTok/Instagram Reels
- Post 1-2 skit clips daily as Reels/TikToks
- Caption: "AI made this skit 🤯 Full version on ShortReels"
- Use trending sounds and hashtags

### Reddit
- Post to r/artificial, r/AIart, r/InternetIsBeautiful
- Title: "I built an app where AI generates comedy skits"

### Product Hunt
- Launch day: Get upvotes, drive install spike
- Prepare maker comment explaining the app
